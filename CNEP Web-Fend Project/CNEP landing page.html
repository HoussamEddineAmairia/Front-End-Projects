Frequently Ask Questions

Can I accept both Paypal and Stripe?

Yes, you can accept payments through both PayPal and Stripe for online transactions, giving your clients flexible and secure options.

What available is refund period?

Our standard refund period is 14 days from the date of the transaction. Refunds are subject to our terms and conditions.

Where are you from?

CNEP Bank is proudly based in Algeria, serving individuals and businesses nationwide with modern banking services.

What are your opening hours?

Our branches are open Sunday to Thursday, 8:30 AM – 3:30 PM. Online banking is available 24/7.

How can I open an account with CNEP Bank?

You can open an account by visiting your nearest CNEP Bank branch with valid ID and proof of residence. Online account opening will be available soon.

Is online banking secure?

Yes, our online banking platform uses advanced encryption and two-factor authentication to keep your information safe.

Can I manage my account from a mobile app?

Absolutely. Our mobile banking app allows you to check balances, transfer funds, pay bills, and more—anytime, anywhere.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, all our fees are transparent and clearly listed in our pricing plans. You’ll always know what you’re paying for.
